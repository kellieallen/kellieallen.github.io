Kellie Allen

Front End Developer

After years of working closely with a dev team while in product management at a digital communications company, I finally decided I wanted to transition to the tech side.

With a 9 year stint in NYC, I've returned home to Indianapolis and am excited to join its burgeoning tech community where I'm currently a web developer and Certified Salesforce Administrator. I've found a passion for using data to solve problems with creative and thoughtful solutions.

When I'm not coding, I challenge myself to read 24 books per year. I also enjoy canoeing, cooking and maintaining a database of favorite recipes.

I have experience building projects with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, jQuery, Postman, PostgreSQL, GitHub, Cascade CMS, PoliEngine, Wordpress, Squarespace and Heroku.
